# Cycle Positioning Rules

## Five-Dimensional Scoring System

Each dimension scored 0-10. Composite score is weighted average.

### Economic Cycle (weight: 15%)
| Score | Indicator State |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-2 | GDP contracting >2%, PMI < 40, unemployment > 8% |
| 3-4 | GDP growth < 1%, PMI 40-48, unemployment rising |
| 5 | GDP growth 1-3%, PMI 48-52, unemployment stable |
| 6-7 | GDP growth 3-5%, PMI 52-58, unemployment falling |
| 8-10 | GDP growth >5%, PMI > 60, unemployment < 3.5% |

### Profit Cycle (weight: 15%)
| Score | Indicator State |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-2 | Earnings declining >20%, margins collapsing, negative revisions |
| 3-4 | Earnings flat to -10%, margins compressing, mostly negative revisions |
| 5 | Earnings growing 0-10%, margins stable, mixed revisions |
| 6-7 | Earnings growing 10-20%, margins expanding, positive revisions |
| 8-10 | Earnings growing >20%, margins at peak, uniformly positive revisions |

### Psychology Pendulum (weight: 20%)
| Score | Indicator State |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-2 | VIX > 35, fund outflows extreme, media panic, fear/greed < 15 |
| 3-4 | VIX 25-35, moderate outflows, cautious media tone |
| 5 | VIX 18-25, neutral flows, balanced media |
| 6-7 | VIX 13-18, moderate inflows, optimistic media |
| 8-10 | VIX < 13, extreme inflows, euphoric media, fear/greed > 85 |

### Risk Attitude Cycle (weight: 20%)
| Score | Indicator State |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-2 | Credit spreads > 90th percentile, IPO market frozen, covenant quality very strong |
| 3-4 | Credit spreads above median, low IPO activity, strong covenants |
| 5 | Credit spreads at median, normal IPO activity, standard covenants |
| 6-7 | Credit spreads below median, active IPO market, weakening covenants |
| 8-10 | Credit spreads < 10th percentile, IPO frenzy, covenant-lite dominant |

### Credit Cycle (weight: 30%)
| Score | Indicator State |
|-------|----------------|
| 0-2 | Lending standards very tight, defaults rising, credit scarce |
| 3-4 | Lending standards tightening, defaults elevated, selective lending |
| 5 | Normal lending standards, average defaults, balanced supply-demand |
| 6-7 | Lending standards loosening, defaults low, abundant credit |
| 8-10 | Lending standards very loose, defaults minimal, "money is free" |

## Action Rules

### Composite Score 0-3: AGGRESSIVE
- Maximum exposure to risk assets (90-100%)
- Favor cyclical and value stocks
- Reduce cash to minimum
- Accept higher volatility
- Look for quality companies at distressed prices

### Composite Score 4-5: MODERATELY AGGRESSIVE
- Above-average risk exposure (70-80%)
- Blend of value and growth
- Some cash buffer (10-15%)
- Tilt toward undervalued sectors

### Composite Score 6: NEUTRAL
- Balanced allocation (60-65% risk assets)
- Diversified portfolio
- Reasonable cash position (15-20%)
- Focus on quality and fair value

### Composite Score 7-8: MODERATELY DEFENSIVE
- Below-average risk exposure (40-50%)
- Favor defensive sectors (utilities, staples, healthcare)
- Significant cash position (25-35%)
- Tighten margin of safety requirements

### Composite Score 9-10: DEFENSIVE
- Minimum risk exposure (20-30%)
- Maximum quality focus
- Large cash position (40%+)
- Consider hedging
- Wait for better opportunities
